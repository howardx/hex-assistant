# Tech News — Scope & Format

Defines what "the news" means when hex2 asks what's new or for a digest.

- **Domains:** AI, crypto, and tech broadly.
- **Sources to scan:** Twitter (X), Reddit, podcasts, and the open web.
- **Goal:** Surface what's genuinely new and important, then summarize it. Separate signal from noise.
- **Default format:** Grouped by domain (AI / crypto / tech). Each item a tight bullet with a one-line takeaway. Lead with the most important.
- Flag genuinely important items distinctly from routine updates.
